Profile of FLAT

VISION

To form a foreign community user and devloper as well as to introduce foreign cultures to UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta's environment


MISION

  • Motivating student of UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta to know, to learn and to develop.
  • Helping and serving students of UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta to master foreign language.
  • Seeking and exploring talents and skills from UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta's students.
  • Creating foreign languages environment in UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta, which is aimed to face the global challenges.
  • Bringing UKM Bahasa-FLAT to be the center of student activities on foreign languages and cultures field.
  • Introducing the world's culture diversity through foreign languages to UIN Syarif Hidayatullah Jakarta's environment.
